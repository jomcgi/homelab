## CRDs

The following CRDs from extensions.agents.x-k8s.io are bundled in the agent-sandbox subchart
(charts/agent-sandbox/crds/) and are installed automatically by Helm before templates are rendered:

  - sandboxes.agents.x-k8s.io
  - sandboxclaims.extensions.agents.x-k8s.io
  - sandboxtemplates.extensions.agents.x-k8s.io
  - sandboxwarmpools.extensions.agents.x-k8s.io

No separate CRD install step is required.

## agent-sandbox controller

The agent-sandbox controller is deployed as part of this release (agent-sandbox subchart).
It runs in the release namespace and reconciles Sandbox, SandboxClaim, SandboxTemplate,
and SandboxWarmPool objects.
