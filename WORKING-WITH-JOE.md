# Working with Joe

**Joe McGinley** — Senior Platform Engineer, Vancouver (PST)

---

## What I do

Internal tooling and infrastructure. CI/CD, observability, deployment pipelines, developer platforms. My job is to make your job easier to do.

## Communicating with me

Async by default. Include a link to the code, PR, trace ID, or relevant doc. That gets a faster response than a meeting invite. If it's urgent, flag it as urgent.

Meetings need an agenda. If it could've been a message, it should've been.

Always available to pair. Good for debugging and unfamiliar territory.

## Giving me work

Start with the problem, not the solution. I'll push back if I think there's a simpler path.

Context up front saves time: a doc, a diagram, a Slack thread, whatever you have. I'll move faster and ask fewer questions.

## Feedback

I'm direct — in PRs, in Slack, wherever. Comments are about the work. I expect the same back.

The goal is to reach the right decision quickly. If you see a problem or disagree, raise it early. I'll do the same.

I'll ask about observability, failure modes, and rollback plans. These questions come up early because they're expensive to answer late.
